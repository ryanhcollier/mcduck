Strat 03

Covered Call Protocol

IDLE
  • Price > SMA50 & SMA200
  • MACD > Signal Line
  • RSI14: 35 - 55 (Neutral)
Why it works
Generates income on long-term holdings strictly when momentum (MACD) is positive but the asset is not overbought (Neutral RSI), maximizing premium without capping near-term upside bursts.

Exit Rules
Roll at 21 DTE or if the short call is tested, capturing theta decay while maintaining the core stock position.
